Welcome to FamilyGroup Foundation

We are building a better future through health, education, and community development, making a real impact in the Democratic Republic of Congo and beyond.

About Us

FamilyGroup Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to social and medical transformation. Our goal is to provide access to quality medical care, modern education, and a dignified future for families in Africa.

Our Projects

  • Creation of an international medical laboratory in Kinshasa
  • Support for vulnerable families
  • Education and entrepreneurship program for youth
  • Distribution of community health kits

Our Partners

We collaborate with hospitals, universities, NGOs, and international institutions committed to sustainable development and public health.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Transparency and ethics in all our initiatives.
  • Solidarity: Putting people first, building together.
  • Excellence: Providing quality services for lasting results.
  • Hope: Restoring faith in a better future.

Our Team

Our team is composed of passionate professionals from various fields: healthcare, education, engineering, entrepreneurship, and social work. Together, we work to build a strong and united Africa.
